FAQ

  • 1. Do I need a Twitter account?

    • Yes, in order to use our services you must have a valid and active Twitter account.

  • 2. How to login into Twitter?

    • Right-click on the 'Login with Twitter' button, located on the left side menu and login using your Twitter credentials.

  • 3. Can I use the tools on a newly created account?

    • Yes, as long as the Twitter account contains data to be analyzed. For example, posts, comments, and likes.

  • 4. For how long does my analyzed data is retained?

    • Social Media Analytics does not retain data. The analyzed content is private and will be deleted once your session is over.

  • 5. Can I use multiple Twitter accounts simultaneously?

    • No, you can only use one Twitter account simultaneosly. If you would like to use another account, logout from your current Twitter account and use new credentials.

  • 6. Can I download my analyzed data?

    • No, the content shown in this application is for reviewing exclusively.

  • 7. Can I share my results with 3rd party applications?

    • No, downloading and distributing the analyzed data to third party applications goes against our policies.

  • 8. Is the service free?

    • Yes, our service is completelly free of charges.
